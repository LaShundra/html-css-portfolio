About Me

I am a single mother of two. I started exploring my creativity and passion for designing web pages when I was in my early 30s. I was afraid to venture off into tech career with fear of not having the skills. My oldest son passed away in 2019 and my mourning for him opened the door for me to delve deeper into my creative side. I started self-training with various web developer programs with the hopes of gaining enough skills to obtain a career. This is where my journey begins.
